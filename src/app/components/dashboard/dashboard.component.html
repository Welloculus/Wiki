What is Welloculus?
Welloculus is a complete health monitoring solution that is useful for both patients and their healthcare providers in connecting various health monitors to single platform and view the health statistics of one self or many patients at same time. Various actions are connected with this monitoring like emergency notifications and statistical analysis of overall health of all patients connected to a healthcare provider.
What does it mean?
A combination of two words, Well and Oculus, which means to keep an eye on the wellness of those associated to it, Welloculus is designed to serve multiple entities associated to healthcare over single platform.
What can it do for you?
All health conscious people, if you are using health monitoring gadgets but are tired of constant peeking into the device or your mobile, all you need is Welloculus app and you won’t even need to take your reports to your doctor from next time! Welloculus will keep an eye on you with the help of your monitoring devices and notify you, your emergency contacts and even your doctor in case of a risk. So even if you do not realize the health criticality or are not in a situation to run for help, Welloculus will be there to run for help for you!
About Welloculus
Welloculus is designed to care about the health of its users not only in a day-to-day life, but also when they need it the most, that is in emergency situations. We are involved in complete lifecycle of healthcare monitoring from keeping an eye on the vital statistics of patients to analysis and reporting for the betterment of society on larger picture. As our name indicates, we proctor the well-being of every individual who is linked to us via their mobile and health monitoring devices.
