Wellie

Technology stack

Wellie is created using some of the latest and buzzing technologies like:

  • Android – this needs no introduction!
  • Android speech recognizer – This gives Wellie a capability to understand what you say
  • Android text to speech – Wellie uses this to convert all its actions to speech so that you don’t have to leave your work and read everything. You can just listen to it
  • API.AI – a human-computer interaction technology that bridges the gap between you and your own home assistant
  • Sprint Boot – a strong server side framework to empower Wellie with speed and security for your real-time and personal environment
Cockpit
A complete web based solution for Welloculus facilitators, healthcare providers and device suppliers where each user can perform various operations based upon their roles and manage their association with Welloculus effectively to make the most use of it.
IOT Gateway
A gateway to connect with multiple health monitoring devices via Raspberry Pi or mobile application. The gateway will receive data from various devices and channels and transfer it to relevant endpoint for further action.
